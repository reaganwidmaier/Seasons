    Fall: Thanksgiving

    Fall is one of the most wonderful seasons. One of the best holidays occurs in the fall: Thanksgiving!
    Thanksgiving is a holiday to give thanks and spend time with your family. It is always on the
    fourth Thursday of November. This year, 2017, Thanksgiving is November 23rd. At Thanksgiving dinner, there are so many
    delicious foods and deserts! My favorite food is mashed potatoes and my favorite desert is banana cream pie.
